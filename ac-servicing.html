Our 5-Point Maintenance Process

Professional precision at every step of our AC diagnostic check.

01

Initial Audit & Test

We check temperature levels, airflow speed, and monitor compressor sounds to diagnose hidden faults.

02

Jet Wet Washing

We wrap the indoor AC unit in a clean plastic service bag and wash the filters, blower wheel, and coils using high-pressure jet pumps.

03

Drainage Flush

The drain tray and water exit pipe are high-pressure flushed to clear blockage debris, preventing messy water drip leaks inside.

04

Gas Pressure Vetting

Our technicians check refrigerant pressures using gauge manifolds, performing leakage repair and gas top-up if necessary.

05

Performance & Warranty Check

We run the unit for 10 minutes, measuring grid current and grill output temperature. Includes a 30-day service warranty protection.
