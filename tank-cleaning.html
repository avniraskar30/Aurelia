Our 6-Stage Process

Our systematic method ensures 100% sterile results every single time.

01

Mechanized Dewatering

We pump out the remaining water and mud using heavy-duty submersible sludge pumps, preparing the tank walls.

02

Sludge & Vacuum Removal

High-powered industrial vacuum machines suck out remaining sludge, mud, and sand from the tank bed.

03

High-Pressure Jet Washing

Heavy-duty jet machines blast off mold, algae, calcified scaling, and rust stains from the walls and ceiling.

04

Chemical Scrubbing & Antialgae Treatment

Food-grade, non-hazardous cleaning solutions are scrubbed onto the surfaces to destroy deep-seated moss roots.

05

Vacuum Suction & Rinsing

The tank is thoroughly rinsed with fresh water and vacuumed again to ensure absolutely no chemical residue remains.

06

UV Disinfection (Optional)

An ultra-violet radiator lamp is suspended inside the empty tank to eliminate any airborne bacteria or spores before refilling.
