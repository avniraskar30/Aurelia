What We Offer

Finding trustworthy and productive workforce solutions can be challenging. Aurelia Group streamlines your operations by supplying background-checked, physically fit, and strictly trained security guards and housekeeping personnel.

24/7 Security Guard Supply

Licensed and physically fit security guards, society watchmen, gate-keepers, and night-shift patrol crew to safeguard your parameters against unauthorized access.

Professional Housekeeping Crews

Janitorial and cleaning staff trained in deep-cleaning chemistry, floor scrubbing machinery, waste disposal, and general maintenance.

Background Vetting & ID Check

Every candidate undergoes detailed reference checks, identity verification, and strict background screens to ensure complete safety for your site.

Our Recruitment & Placement Process

Our workflow guarantees the highest standard of staff integrity.

01

Requirement Analysis

We work with you to understand the exact job profiles, shifts, uniform requirements, and work scopes needed.

02

Sourcing & Vetting

We screen candidates from our database, performing thorough background check screening, ID verification, and reference audits.

03

Security & Skill Training

Janitors undergo chemical and machinery training; security guards undergo fitness, patrol, fire safety, and gate registers logging checks.

04

On-Site Deployment

We place our staff at your site under uniform instructions, introducing site rules and logs registers on day one.

05

Supervisor Vetting

Our area supervisors conduct surprise checks, auditing attendance registers and performance to maintain peak efficiency.
